About Us

The May Foundation is a youth-led nonprofit organization that strives to empower the underprivileged through education, health, and economic support. We believe in inclusive growth and work with communities to create sustainable change. Founded with compassion and purpose, we focus on real-world impact and nurturing human potential.

Our Programs

  • Educational Empowerment: Providing tools, mentorship, and scholarships to underprivileged children.
  • Women's Hygiene: Menstrual hygiene awareness campaigns and product distribution.
  • Financial Literacy: Sessions on savings, budgeting, and entrepreneurship for youth and families.
  • Community Development: Clean-up drives, health camps, infrastructure improvements, and sustainability awareness.
